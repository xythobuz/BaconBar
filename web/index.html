BaconBar

Quickly open Reddit links and get notified about new PMs, right from your Mac system menu bar.

Requires Mac OS X 10.8 or newer

Full control

View links from your Frontpage or only show specific subreddits. You can also specify how many links you want to see at once.

Orangered

Get notified about new PMs or replies to your posts and comments. The refresh interval is configurable!
